Aniwat Phaphuangwittayakul

Assistant Professor & Lecturer & AI Researcher

International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University

My research focuses on the intersection of computer vision, complex networks, and generative models. I am particularly interested in few-shot learning methods, knowledge discovery from large-scale data, and developing practical AI systems for real-world applications including medical imaging and transportation.

Research Interests

  • Computer Vision
    Image processing, object detection, visual recognition, and transformer-based architectures.
  • Complex Networks
    Node importance identification, skeleton network extraction, and graph-based modeling.
  • Knowledge Discovery
    Extracting meaningful patterns from large-scale datasets using data mining techniques.
  • Generative Models
    GANs, VAEs, and hybrid generative architectures for data synthesis.
  • Few-Shot Learning
    Meta-learning and prototypical approaches for learning from limited examples.
